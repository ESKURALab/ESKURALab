About

A laboratory for adaptive and useful robotics

ESKURA Lab is a university robotics laboratory focused on Robotics.

Our research combines robot learning, manipulation, perception, knowledge representation, and human-robot interaction to build systems that can transfer skills across tasks and domains.

Research lines

What we work on

01

Robot Learning

Learning reusable skills from demonstrations, interaction, simulation, and real-world experience.

02

Embodied Knowledge

Connecting symbolic knowledge, sensor data, and robot actions for adaptive decision-making.

03

Manipulation

Planning and control methods for robust grasping, tool use, assembly, and physical interaction.

04

Human-Robot Collaboration

Robots that understand users, communicate clearly, and support people in shared environments.
